Project Overview

The Asset Management System is designed to solve the critical challenge of tracking and managing physical and digital assets across large organizations. From procurement to disposal, the system handles the complete asset lifecycle with automated workflows, real-time tracking, maintenance scheduling, and comprehensive reporting.

Built with modern technologies and scalability in mind, the platform supports multi-location asset tracking, barcode/QR code integration, automated depreciation calculations, maintenance reminders, and role-based access control for different organizational levels.
